Privacy Policy

Okay, here's a more human-sounding version of that privacy policy:

**1. Intro**

Your privacy is a big deal to us. This policy explains how we handle your info when you sign up for our box subscription.

**2. What Info We Grab**

From You:

*   Name, email, phone number, and what you use to log in.
*   Where to ship and bill you.
*   What you like in your boxes (sizes, categories, product types, etc.).
*   What you've ordered before.
*   Your feedback, reviews, and ratings of stuff.

Payment Info:

*   Credit card stuff (safely processed by payment companies).
*   When you paid, when you're due to pay, and if you've renewed.

Techy Stuff:

*   Your IP address, what kind of device you are using, the browser, and your operating system.
*   What you look at on our site, what you like in boxes, and items in your cart.
*   Cookies and other tracking things.

**3. How We Use Your Information**

We use your info to:

*   Get your boxes to you.
*   Handle your account, payments, and renewals.
*   Make your boxes just how you want them.
*   Send you confirmations, shipping news, and our newsletter.
*   Help you out if you need it.
*   Suggest products you might dig.
*   See what people are buying and make things better.
*   Stop fraud and weird stuff.

**4. Who We Share Data With**

We might share with:

*   Payment companies: To deal with payments safely.
*   Shipping folks: To get you your boxes.
*   Marketing people: To send you emails about deals (if you said it was okay).
*   Analytics folks: To learn how people use our site and fix it.
*   The Law: Only if we have to.

We don't sell your info to advertisers.

**5. Cookies and Tracking**

Cookies help us:

*   Keep you logged in.
*   Remember what you like in your boxes and in your cart.
*   See how people use our site.
*   Show you stuff you might like.

You can change your cookie settings in your browser.

**6. How Long We Keep Your Data**

We keep your data as long as you have an account or as long as we need to for billing, support, or legal stuff. You can delete your account whenever you want.

**7. Your Rights**

You can:

*   See and change your personal or subscription info.
*   Cancel your subscription and dump your account.
*   Ask for a copy of your data.
*   Say no to promotional emails.
*   Mess with your cookies and settings.

**8. How We Keep Your Data Safe**

We use encryption and security to protect your data. But no system is perfect, so keep your login info safe.

**9. Kids' Privacy**

Our service is not for kids under 13. We don't knowingly grab data from them.

**10. Policy Changes**

We might change this policy sometimes. We'll post updates on this page.
